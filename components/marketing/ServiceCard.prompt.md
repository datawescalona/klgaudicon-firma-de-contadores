The grid-breaking workhorse of KLG marketing pages: line-art icon tile, title, blurb, and a lifting "ver servicio" link.

```jsx
<ServiceCard icon={<i data-lucide="search-check"></i>} title="Auditoría Financiera"
  tone="cobalt" tilt badge={<Badge tone="cyan">Control interno</Badge>}>
  Razonabilidad de estados financieros y detección de fraudes.
</ServiceCard>
```

Alternate `tilt` and `tilt="alt"` across a grid to break rigidity. Reserve `tone="bronze"` for the conversion-driving "gancho" service.
